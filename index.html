Sebastian Stan

Actor

August 13th, 1982 (36 years old)

Sebastian Stan is a Romanian-American Television and Movie actor. He was born in Constanta, Romania, but moved to Vienna, Austria with his mother when he was 8 years old after the Romanian Revolution. When he was twelve years old, they moved to Rockland County, New York, where his mother remarried.

Stan participated in many school plays and attended acting summer camps, which cultivated his love of acting. He attended Rutgers University's Mason Gross School of the Arts and studied abroad at Shakespeare's Globe Theater in London.
